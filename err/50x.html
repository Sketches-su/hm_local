50x Some Sort of an Internal Server Error

Most frequent reasons:

  • errors in .htaccess (typos, using disabled/missing Apache modules, etc.)
  • fatal errors in PHP scripts (exceeding memory_limit, running out of max_execution_time, etc.)

In most cases this problem comes from the backend (Apache or PHP), not nginx.

Please look through /path/to/your/webhost/logs/*-error.log to find what happened.
